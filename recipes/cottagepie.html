Cottage Pie



Make a Cottage Pie


Ingredients
  • 4 Potatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 1 Mid Sized Cottage
  • 1 Pie

Steps
  1. Take the potatoes, carrots and the minced beef, put in a bag and throw in the bin, you will not be needing these
  2. Get the mid sized cottage and carefully place it in the pie
  3. Put the pie, with the cottage inside, into the oven on quite a high temperature
  4. When that cottage pie looks ready, take it out of the oven
  5. Now you are free to eat the cottage pie whenever you want
